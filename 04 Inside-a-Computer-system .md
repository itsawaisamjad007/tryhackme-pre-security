
  Introduction:-
                In this we will learn the basic component of computer

   Inside the computer :-
                        * Motherboard :-
                                      skeleton and nerves & it
                        is like a human skeleton and nerves system
                        which hold and connect all component of
                        computer, it consist of CPU Socket ,RAM
                        slot and expansion slot and other ports.
                        Any other component plug or connect through
                        motherboard


                        * RAM :-
                                Short-term memory & store data temporarily
                         when system is running . Modern RAM modules use
                         technologies like DDR5 or DDR6 for increased speed
                          and performance.

                        * PSU :-
                                 Heart and Lung & Power Supply Unit .It
                         supply power to all components of computer

                        * CPU :-
                                The Brain & stand for central processing
                         unit or processor . It execute and calculate
                         instructions . Modern computer haver multiple number
                         of core on it.

                        * HARD Disk :-
                                     Long term memory & to store data permanent
                         SDDs and HDDs .HDDs use older technology with moving
                         parts, limiting performance. SSDs have no moving parts
                         and use memory chips, allowing much faster speeds. HDDs
                         remain popular for their large capacity at low cost.

                        * Network Adaptor :-
                                            Communicate with other computer and
                         network.  Network adapters come in wireless and wired
                          variants. Often they're embedded in the motherboard,
                          but they can also be added as expansion cards. Network
                          cards typically connect via PCI Express ports.

                        * Graphic Card :-
                                            To Show videos , games and editing
                         on screen . If we have no GPU our screen will be
                         blank or very slow

                         *I/O Devices :-
                                        To send and receive data to and from
                          computer
